Best Slow Cooker Hot Chocolate

Description

This decadent crockpot hot chocolate is easy to make with milk, condensed milk, cream, chocolate chips, and vanilla in the slow cooker. Yields 10 servings

Ingredients

  • 6 cups milk
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 (14oz) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine milk, chocolate chips, cream, condensed milk and vaniola in a slow cooker
  2. Cover and cook on Low, stirring occasionally, until chocolate chips are melted and mixture is smooth, about 2 hours.
